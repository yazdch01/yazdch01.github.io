Who I am

My name is Cheragh Ali Yazdani and I was born on October 24th in Tangi siyah village of Yakawlang District of Bamyan province of Afghanistan. Due decades of war I have experienced leaving in different countries as Pakistan, Iran and India. I started my primary school in Iran and ended up getting in one of the top international schools in Afghanistan (Ariana Afghan-Turk High school).

In 2012 I applied for United World College schools and after passing three examination could get in to UWC Maastricht(Link). In 2 years of studying in Netherlands I had the opportunity to visit different European countries and also different people such as the King of Netherlands. Overall, UWC gave me a good experience of living in a world family.

In 2013 I applied for Luther College in United States, and luckily could get in to this college for class of 2018. I am having a happy life here at Luther College, aiming to major in Computer Science and minor in Economics.
